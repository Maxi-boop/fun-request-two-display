What are Fun Requests?

Most web applications are made using tons of frameworks and libraries, which makes Web Development look hard. Of course, WebDeb is hard, but the fundamentals are just as important as learning something like Webpack.

Fun Requests are a way for StuySpec developers to practice their skills in a fun way! These small webpages test the basics of HTML, CSS, and JS, without the frills.
